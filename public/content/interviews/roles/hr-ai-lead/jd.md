## A Snapshot of Your Day

You start your day reviewing an early prototype from the HR AI team. You pair with one of the engineers on how to better ground an assistant in the company's HR knowledge, iterate on a prompt, inspect failure cases together, and sharpen the evaluation set.

Mid-morning you are in a working session with HR stakeholders. You translate a real business pain point into a concrete AI solution concept with a realistic build plan, clear data requirements, and a path to production. You listen, challenge assumptions, and leave the room with a decision.

In the afternoon you switch hats: a mentoring conversation with a team member in India, a technical design review on retrieval and agent orchestration, a quick alignment with Cyber Security and Data Privacy on guardrails for an upcoming pilot. You close the day by sketching the next quarter of the HR AI roadmap together with international HR AI leadership, balancing quick wins, foundational building blocks, and a few bolder bets.

- **Contribute to the HR AI roadmap**: sequence quick wins, foundational platforms, and strategic bets that reimagine major HR processes.
